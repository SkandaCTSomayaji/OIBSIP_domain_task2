Why I Admire Him

In my own words:

Swami Vivekananda was a profound thinker and a man of action who brilliantly bridged ancient Eastern spirituality with modern Western thought. He was a key figure in the introduction of the Indian philosophies of Vedanta and Yoga to the world.

His message of universal acceptance, selfless service, and the potential for every individual to achieve greatness is a timeless source of inspiration. He challenged people to find the divine within themselves and to serve others as a form of worship.

"Arise, awake, and stop not till the goal is reached."

— Swami Vivekananda
